<b>What is Telangana Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) for SC/ST/BC/Disabled Welfare 2019-20?</b>

The Government of Telangana invites applications for Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) for SC/ST/BC/Disabled Welfare students 2019-20 from students of Telangana state studying in class 11 to PhD level. Initiated by the Department of Backward Class Welfare, this scholarship aims to allow class 10 passed students to pursue post matric studies such as intermediate, ITI, polytechnic, professional courses, graduate and postgraduate courses, Ph.D., etc. The benefits of this scholarship are provided in the form of fee reimbursement. 

<b>Who is offering this scholarship ?</b>

This scholarship is offered by the Government of Telangana.

<b>Who can apply for this scholarship ?</b>

* The students who are domicile of Telangana state can apply for this scholarship. <br>
* The students must belong to SC/ST/BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category.<br>
* They must have passed class 10 and pursuing studies at the post-matric level.<br>
* Students belonging to SC/ST category must have an annual parental income of less than INR 2 Lakhs.<br>
* Students belonging to BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category who belong to rural areas must have an annual parental income of less than INR 1.5 Lakhs.<br>
  Students belonging to BC/EBC/Disabled Welfare category who belong to urban areas must have an annual parental income of less than INR 2 Lakhs.<br>
* Students must have passed class 10 examination with a minimum of 75% attendance at the end of each quarter.

<b>What are the benefits ?</b>

The benefits of the scholarship will be in the form of fee reimbursement. The tuition fee reimbursement will be dependent on the course of study. Furthermore, the selected scholars will also receive variable maintenance or mess charges on a monthly basis.

<h2>Application Procedure</h2>

<b>Documents required for the Telangana Post Matric Scholarship (PMS) for SC/ST/BC/Disabled Welfare 2019-20.</b>

* Latest passport size photograph.<br>
* Aadhaar card of the student. <br>
* Bank passbook first-page showing student's name, Bank Account Number and IFSC code of the Bank Branch (Not required for EBC students).<br>
* Study/bonafide certificate for the last 7 consecutive years of study.<br>
* CET (Common Entrance Test) allotment order for CET courses.

<b>Follow the below steps to apply:</b>

<b>Step 1</b>: Visit the <a href="https://telanganaepass.cgg.gov.in/">Official EPASS website.</a>. <br>
<b>Step 2</b>: Scroll down and select <b>Post Matric Scholarship Services.</b><br>
<b>Step 3</b>: Click On Fresh Registration under <b>Postmatric Scholarships For Fresh Registration</b>.<br>
<b>Step 4</b>: Read The instructions and tick all the checkboxes of required documents and proceed.<br>
<b>Step 5</b>: Click on 'Apply Now'.<br>
<b>Step 6</b>: Enter the details of the SSC (class 10) examination.<br>
<b>Step 7</b>: After the verification of SSC credentials, complete the application procedure and upload the necessary documents.<br>
