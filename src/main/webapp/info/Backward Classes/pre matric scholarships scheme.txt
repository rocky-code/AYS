<b>What is Telangana Pre-Matric Scholarships For SC/ST/BC/Disabled Welfare 2019-20?</b>

The Government of Telangana invites applications for Pre Matric Scholarships for SC/ST/BC/EBC and Disabled Welfare Students 2019-20. The aim of this scheme is to minimize the incidence of drop out by supporting SC/ST/BC/EBC/Disabled welfare students studying in classes 9 and 10. The selected scholars will receive variable awards.

<b>Who is offering this scholarship ?</b>

This scholarship is being provided by the Government of Telangana.

<b>Who can apply for this scholarship ?</b>

* Students studying in classes 9 and 10 can apply.<br>
* Students must be a domicile of Telangana state.<br>
* Students who belong to the categories of SC/ST must have an annual parental income of INR 2,00,000 (Two Lakhs) or below.<br.
* BC & EBC and Minority Welfare Students who belong to the rural area family must have an annual parental income of INR 1,50,000 (One Lakh Fifty Thousand) or below.<br>
BC & EBC and Minority Welfare Students who belong to the urban area family must have an annual parental income of INR 2,00,000 (Two Lakhs) or below.Disabled welfare students must have an annual parental income of INR 1,00,000( 1 Lakh) or below.<br>
* Students must also have an attendance of 75% at the end of each quarter.

<b>What are the benefits ?</b>

Variable monetary awards will be disbursed to all eligible students with respect to the state corpus of the scholarship funds.

<b>Documents required for the Telangana Pre-Matric Scholarships For SC/ST/BC/Disabled Welfare 2019-20.</b>

For the registration form, the applicants have to submit:- <br>
* Scanned Passport size image of the applicant student.<br>
* Scanned bank passbook of the students account (first page) or cancelled cheque (of the same account).<br>
* Scanned UID (Aadhar Card) of the applicantFor verification, the applicants might be required to submit the following documents at a later stage:- <br>
* Bonafide certificate<br>
* Caste certificate.<br>
* Income certificate.

<b>Follow the below steps to apply:</b>

<b>Step 1</b>: Visit the <a href="https://telanganaepass.cgg.gov.in/">Official EPASS website.</a>. <br>
<b>Step 2</b>: Scroll down and select <b>Pre Matric Scholarship Services.</b><br>
<b>Step 3</b>: Click On Fresh Registration under <b>Pre Matric Scholarships For Fresh Registration</b>.<br>
<b>Step 4</b>: Read The instructions and tick all the checkboxes of required documents and proceed.<br>
<b>Step 5</b>: Fill in all required details.<br>
<b>Step 6</b>: Upload the required documents and submit.<br>
