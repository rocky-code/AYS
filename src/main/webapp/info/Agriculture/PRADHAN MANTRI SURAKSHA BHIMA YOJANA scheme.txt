
Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana is a government-backed accident insurance scheme in India. It was originally mentioned in the 2015 Budget speech by Finance Minister Late Arun Jaitley in February 2015.[1] It was formally launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 8 May in Kolkata.

This scheme is implemented by the Department of Agril. Marketing to the Market functionaries of Agril. Market Committees in the state.

<h2>Salient features of the Scheme are as under:-</h2>

<b>Eligibility</b> :- All savings bank account holders in the age group of 18 years (completed) to 70 years (age nearer birthday) with Aadhaar number linked to that account can give a simple consent form to the bank every year before 1st of June in order to join the scheme. In case of multiple saving bank accounts held by an individual in one or different banks, the persons  would be eligible to join the scheme through any one savings bank account only. Name of the nominee to be given in the form.

<b>Premium</b>:-  Rs. 12/- per annum (Plus service tax as applicable).

<b>Payment</b>:-  The Premium will directly be auto-debited by the bank from the subscribers  account. This is the only mode available.

<b>Risk Coverage</b>:- 

a) For accidental death and total disablement -  Rs. 2.00 Lakhs : Total irrecoverable loss of both eyes or loss of use of both hands or feet or loss of sight of one eye and loss of use of hand or foot.<br>
b) For partial disablement - Rs. 1.00 Lakhs : Total and irrecoverable loss of sight of one eye or loss of use of one hand or foot.

<b>Term of Risk</b>:- A person has to opt for the scheme every year. He can also prefer to give a long term option of continuing in which case his account will be auto debited every year by the bank.

The Customers can link their existing Savings Bank Account with AADHAR before opting PMSBY scheme. New customers are also eligible to join the scheme by seeding their AADHAR number to Savings Bank account. However KYC/Due Diligence have to be followed.

<h2>Documents Required:</h2>

Mentioned below is a list of documents required to be a part of the PMSBY.

<b>Form</b>- Submission of the duly filled PMSBY application form containing details such as name, contact details, Aadhaar number, and details of the selected nominee. This form is available in several regional languages apart from English and Hindi. For example, the form is available in Marathi, Tamil, Oriya, etc.<br>
<b>Aadhar Card</b>- In case the applicants Aadhaar card details are not linked to the stated savings bank account, the applicant will have to submit a copy of the Aadhaar card. The same will have to be accompanied with the application form.

<h2>Enrol and Register for Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana:</h2>

You can enrol yourself for the PMSBY by approaching an affiliated bank or an insurance company. The Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana form or the PMSBY form can also be downloaded from the governments Jansuraksha website. It is available in multiple languages.<br>

Here is how you can activate the policy using the internet banking facility (PMSBY Online Apply).

<b>Step 1</b> :  Login to the internet banking account.<br>
<b>Step 2 </b>:  Click on Insurance.<br>
<b>Step 3 </b>:  Identify the account to be used for paying the premium amount.<br>
<b>Step 4 </b>:  Check details and confirm.<br>
<b>Step 5 </b>:  Download receipt and note the stated reference number.<br>

<h2>Claim Process of PMSBY:</h2>

You can raise a claim against the PMSBY in case of partial or total disability. In case of death, your nominee can file a claim. If there is no nominee, then the claim amount shall be given to the legal heir of the deceased policyholder. Here is the claim process.

<b>Step 1</b> : Policyholder/nominee should reach out to the bank or the insurance company from where the policy was purchased to raise a claim.<br>
<b>Step 2 </b>: Obtain claim form and fill it. It will include details such as name, address, contact information, details of the hospital, etc. The PMSBY claim form can be downloaded. <br>
<b>For English Claim Form</b>: <a href="https://www.acko.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/pmsby-english-claim-form.pdf">Click Here!</a><br>
<b>For Telugu Claim Form</b>: <a href="https://www.acko.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/pmsby-telugu-claim-form.pdf">Click Here!</a><br>
<b>Step 3 </b>: Submit the filled form with the relevant supporting documentation such as disability certificate or death certificate in case the claim is raised by the nominee.<br>
<b>Step 4 </b>: The insurance company will confirm the details.<br>
<b>Step 5 </b>: If the documents turn out to be proper, the claim amount will be transferred to the specified bank account and the claim will be settled.<br>

<h2>How to Renew PMSBY:</h2>

You can renew PMSBY by enabling the auto-debit facility. This way the premium will be automatically debited from your bank account and the policy shall be renewed. The scheme can be renewed on an annual basis. The policy duration is between June 1 to May 31. Therefore, the policy should be renewed before the end of May. If you do not want to renew, you will have to send a cancellation request to the respective bank.
